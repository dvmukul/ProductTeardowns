## 🏗️ Teardown Framework Template

Whenever I perform a new teardown, I will use the following structured framework to ensure a comprehensive analysis. You can copy this template for each new product teardown.

### 📱 1. Product Overview & Positioning
* **Product Name:** [Product Name]
* **Platform/Version Evaluated:** [e.g., iOS App v2.4, Web Application]
* **Target Audience:** Who is this product built for? Describe the core user segments.
* **Value Proposition:** What is the fundamental promise this product makes to its users? How does it differentiate itself from competitors?
* **Market Context:** What is the competitive landscape? Is it a blue ocean or highly saturated?

### 🎯 2. Core User Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD)
What are the primary "jobs" users are hiring this product to do?
* **Job 1:** [e.g., "When I am traveling, help me find a cheap place to stay so I can spend money on experiences."]
* **Job 2:** [Add additional primary/secondary jobs]
* **Emotional vs. Functional Jobs:** What emotional needs does the product satisfy beyond the functional utility?

### 🎨 3. Key UX Decisions and Why
Analyze the user experience, focusing on flows, friction, and design choices.
* **Onboarding Experience:** How quickly does a user experience the "Aha!" moment? What friction points exist?
* **Core Loop:** Map out the primary actions a user takes repeatedly. Why is it engaging?
* **Brilliant Design Choices:** Highlight specific UI/UX decisions that stood out. Why did the product team design it this way? (Provide hypotheses on their reasoning).
* **Friction Points:** Where is the experience jarring, confusing, or slower than it needs to be?

### ⚙️ 4. Business Model Mechanics
How does this product generate value and capture it?
* **Monetization Strategy:** How does the company make money? (e.g., SaaS subscription, transaction fees, ads, freemium).
* **Acquisition/Growth Loops:** How does the product acquire new users reliably? (e.g., SEO, viral referral loops, paid ads).
* **Retention Hooks:** What features or psychological hooks keep users coming back?

### 💡 5. Recommendations & Takeaways
Imagine you are the PM for this product. What would you do next?
* **Quick Wins (Low Effort, High Impact):** [e.g., Move the CTA above the fold to increase conversion.]
* **Strategic Bets (High Effort, High Impact):** [e.g., Launch a new B2B tier focused on enterprise collaboration.]
* **Biggest Risks/Threats:** What could derail this product's success in the next 1-2 years?

---

> **Note:** To begin a new teardown, create a new `.md` file in this repository using the name of the product (e.g., `Apple_Notes_Teardown.md`) and copy the framework above!
